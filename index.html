Welcome to the LSTC Quote Wall! This repository exists as a result of our being inspired - partly by the creative genius of a certain "Water Angels" manchurian stall owner and partly by Ibn Batuta, a man famed for his 30 years of travel in the 14th Century across most of the Islamic World. Ibn Batuta's memoirs are still held in high regard as readers can get up close and personal with the debauchery of the top dogs of humanity's past. The Quote Wall aims to fulfill a similar purpose for future LSTC members - AUTs or otherwise digging through the archives, so think of this as a message in a bottle.
